# Shortest honest noun

Label a thing by the plainest word that's still true — no pipeline vocabulary on a founder surface. The card reads "Payers" and "Providers priced", never the internal column names behind them.

**Why it exists.** The words that make sense inside the loader are the words that make no sense to a reader. "rate_tins" is a column; "Billing entities" is a fact about the world. Every surface that leaks the former makes the product feel like a database viewer someone forgot to finish.

**How to apply.** Ask what a practitioner would call the thing out loud, then check that the label is still literally true. Shortest wins the tie. Internal names stay in the data dictionary, where they belong.
