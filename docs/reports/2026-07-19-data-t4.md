## 4. 2026-08 Anthem/Empire re-mint: staged tooling had a silent 38-series drop — fixed and proven

t3's `remint-2026-08/mint.sh` + `targets-series.txt` had two compounding bugs
that would have silently dropped **38 of the 79 target series** on the
founder's Aug-1 run:

1. `targets-series.txt` mixed bare series ids (`254_39F0`) with 38 full June
   basenames (`2026-06_042_04B0_in-network-rates.json.gz`) — the awk join
   keys on bare series, so those 38 could never match.
2. The awk needle `_in-network-rates_` (trailing underscore) only matches
   chunked files (`…_1_of_5.json.gz`); all 38 of those series shipped
   **unchunked** in June (`…_in-network-rates.json.gz`) — structurally
   excluded even with (1) fixed, and misreported as "absent from the batch".

Fixed: targets normalized to 79 bare series ids; needle relaxed to
`_in-network-rates` (matches both shapes; `allowed-amounts` files still
skipped). Proven against a fixture extract through the real script in a
sandbox: single-file target matched, chunked target took all chunks,
wrong-month/non-target/allowed-amounts skipped, absent-count correct (77 of
79 with 2 planted). `.harvest/` is gitignored, so the fix is disk-only — the
mint-order README + this report are the durable record.

June-expiring standing: with 39F0 fully banked (§1) nothing else depends on
