## Binding rulings (founder, 2026-07-18 — apply to every item)

- **Exact-rate-first.** Any surface answering a specific question (plan ×
  provider/TIN × code) shows the exact attested figure from
  `provider_rate_signals`/`rate_table_mv`, never a range. A band may appear
  only as (a) one quiet context line under an exact figure, or (b) an
  explicitly-labeled fallback when the specific row doesn't exist. Bands
  relocate to Intelligence (NYS-35); they do NOT appear on today's surfaces.
- **The row count IS the credibility**: 13.4M defined rates, not 50k
  estimates. The index leads with it.
- **Three lenses, one entity**: rate index (org × network × code) ·
  Find-my-plan (patient) · Plans catalog (ops/provider).
- Standing: negotiated rate ≠ out-of-pocket — label "your plan's in-network
  rate · as-of {date}", never "your cost". One H1 per page, in the TopBar.
  Primitives law absolute.
